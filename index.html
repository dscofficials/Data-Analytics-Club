Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join the Data Science Club? +

Simply attend one of our weekly meetings or contact us through the form below. No prior experience required - we welcome beginners!

What are the prerequisites for joining? +

No specific prerequisites! We welcome students from all majors and experience levels. Basic programming knowledge is helpful but not required.

Are there any membership fees? +

Membership is completely free! We occasionally charge small fees for special workshops or events to cover materials and refreshments.

When and where do you meet? +

We meet every Tuesday at 6 PM in the Computer Science building, Room 204. Check our social media for updates on special events.

Can I participate remotely? +

Yes! We offer hybrid participation for most events and maintain active online communities where members can collaborate and share resources.
